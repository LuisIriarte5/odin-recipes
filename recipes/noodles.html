Lasagna Noodles

Description

Making your own noodles has never been easier with the recipe. While it's not necessary considering the wide variety available in supermarkets, the full experience of creating your own ingredients is always rewarding.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Eggs
  • Olive oil
Yes, that's all you need.

Preparation

  1. In a food processor bowl:
    1. Pulse flour and salt together.
    2. Add the egg and oil mixture and pulse until there is no dry flour.
    3. Process for about 1 minute.
    4. Gather the dough into a smooth ball using your hands.
    5. Cover the dough with plastic wrap and let it rest at room temperature for 30 to 60 minutes.
  2. After resting the dough, divide it into 6 equal pieces and ppass them through a pasta machine, rolling them at the thickest setting possible.
