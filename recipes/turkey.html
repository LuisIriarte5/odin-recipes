Thanksgiving Turkey

Description

It is thanksgiving season! So we prepare a special recipe for you to impress your guests. A thanksgiving turkey is a special dish, so you must take your time for doing it, we expect this to be ready in around 3 hour and 30 minutes, but we know you will have the most delicious dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole turkey, neck and giblets removed
  • Aluminium foil
  • Kitchen string
  • 1 bottle of champagne
  • 1 can chicken broth
  • To season the turkey:
    • 2 tablespoons dried parsley
    • 2 tablespoons ground dried rosemary
    • 2 tablespoons rubbed dried sage
    • 2 tablespoons dried thyme leaves
    • 1 tablespoon lemon-pepper seasoning
    • 1 tablespoon salt
    • 2 stalks celery cut into slices
    • 1 medium orange cut in 8 wedges
    • 1 medium onion chopped into large pieces
    • 1 medium carrot cut into slices

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Line a roaster with sheets of aluminium foil long enough to wrap around turkey
  3. Stir parsley, rosemary, sage, thyme, lemon-pepper seasoning and salt together in a small bowl
  4. Rub the mixture into turkey cavity.
  5. Then, stuff with orange wedges, onion, carrot and celery.
  6. Tie turkey legs with kitchen string, then tuck the wings under the body.
  7. Place turkey on the foil in the roasting pan.
  8. Pour champagne and chicken broth over the turkey, making sure to get some liquid in the cavity.
  9. Bring aluminium foil up and over the top of turkey and seal.
  10. Roast turkey in the preheated oven until juices run clear for over 2 1/2 to 3 hours.
  11. Uncover turkey and continue baking until the skin turns golden brown 30 to 60 more minutes. If you have a thermometer, insert it into the thickest part of thigh and it should read 180 degrees F (82 degrees C)
  12. Remove turkey from the oven, cover with two sheets of aluminium foil and allow to rest in a warm area before slice for 10 to 15 minutes.
