Baked Sweet Potatoes

Description

A versatile and delightful recipe using only sweet potatoes. A simple yet delicious dish is presented in this post, guaranteed to become one of your favorites.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 large sweet potatoes
  • 2 pinched dried oregano
  • 2 pinches salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Coat the bottom of a glass or nonstick baking dish with olive oil.
  3. Wash and peel the sweet potatoes, then cut them into medium-sized pieces.
  4. Place the sweet potatoes in the baking dish and stir to coat them with olive oil.
  5. Sprinkle with oregano, salt, and pepper.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until they're soft and fork-tender, which should take about 45 minutes to 1 hour.
