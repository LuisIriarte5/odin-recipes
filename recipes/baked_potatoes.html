Baked Sweet Potatoes

Description

In my opinion the perfect partner for almost all the existing dishes. A very simple and delicious recipe for which we only going to need potatoes is presented in this post. I know this will be one of your favorites when finish.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 large sweet potatoes
  • 2 pinched dried oregano
  • 2 pinches salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Coat the bottom of a glass or nonstick baking dish with olive oil.
  3. Wash and peel sweet potatoes, then cut them into medium pieces.
  4. Place in the baking dish and stir to coat with olive oil.
  5. Sprinkle with oregano, salt and pepper.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until soft and fork-tender, about 45 minutes to 1 hour.
