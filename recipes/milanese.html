Chicken Milanese

Description

A timeless favorite! A chicken milanese is a delightful dish that is both simple and delicious. A personal favorite, it is ready in under 30 minutes and pairs well with a variety of sides.

Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken breast halves
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3/4 cup flour
  • 1 lemon
  • Bread crumbs
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Any vegetable oil

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees F (95 degrees C)
  2. Beat eggs with salt and peper in a shallow dish. Spread flour in another dish and the bred crumbs in a third dish as illustrated in the picture below.
  3. "Butterfly" the chicken breast and follow the next steps
    1. Gently press chicken into flour to coat and shake off any excess.
    2. Dip into beaten eggs.
    3. Press into bread crumbs.
    4. Place breaded chicken on a plate as you repeat the process for the remaining pieces.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat and pan-fry the chicken for 2 to 4 minutes per side. If available, insert a thermometer into the center and it should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C)
  5. Once cooked, transfer the chicken to a banking sheet and keep it warm in the preheated oven while you cook the remaining chicken.
  6. Serve with lemon wedges.
