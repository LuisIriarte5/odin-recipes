Chicken Milanese

Description

A classic milanese chicken is one of my all time favorite plates. It is both simple and delicious. It can be done in less than 30 mimnutes and can be combined with almost everything.

ENJOY!

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken breast halves
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3/4 cup flour
  • 1 lemon
  • Bread crumbs
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Oil (any vegetable oil)

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees F (95 degrees C)
  2. Beat eggs with salt and peper in a shallow dish. Spread flour in another dish and the bred crumbs in a third dish as you can see in the picture below.
  3. "Butterfly" the chicken breast and follow the next steps
    1. Gently press chicken into flour to coat and shake off any excess.
    2. Dip into beaten eggs.
    3. Press into bread crumbs.
    4. Place breaded chicken onto a plate while you repeat the process for the remaining ones.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat and pan-fry chicken 2 to 4 minutes per side. If a termometer is available, instert into the center and it should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C)
  5. Once is ready, transfer the cooked chicken to a banking sheet and keep warm in the preheated oven while cooking the remaining chicken.
  6. Serve with lemon wedges.
