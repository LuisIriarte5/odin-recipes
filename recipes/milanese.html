Chicken Milanese

A classic milanese chicken is one of my all time favorite plates. Its very simple and delicious at the same time. It can be done in less than 30 mimnutes and can be combined with almost everything.

ENJOY!

Ingredients

  • 2 Checken breast halves
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3/4 cup flour
  • 1 lemon
  • Bread crumbs
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
