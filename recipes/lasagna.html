Lasagna

Description

A classic favorite! A good lasagna never disappoints. Though it might not be the simplest dish to prepare, the effort is always rewarded. Be prepared and patient, as it will take around 3 hours to cook.

Let's get started!

Ingredients

This is a very customizable dish, but the essencials are the following:

  • Meat: 3/4 pund ground beef and 1 pound sweet italian sausage.
  • Tomato products: 1 can crushed tomatoes, 2 cans canned tomato sauce and 2 cans tomato paste
  • Cheese: 3/4 pound parmesan, 3/4 pound mozzarella slices and 16 ounces ricotta.
  • Lasagna noodles: We need 12 lasagna noodles. You can buy it or do it yourself.
  • Spices and seasonings: 4 tablespoons fresh parsley, 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil leaves, 1 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1 teaspoon italian seasoning, 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds and 1/4 teaspoon black pepper.
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 egg

Preparation

  1. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in a pan over medium heat until well browned.
  2. Add the tomato products and water. Season with sugar, parsley, basil, salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper. Simmer for about 1 1/2 hours.
  3. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes, then drain and rinse with cold water.
  4. In a mixing boul, combine ricotta cheese with egg, 2 tablespoons of parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon of salt.
  5. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C)
  6. Assemble the dish following these steps:
    1. Spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
    2. Arrange 6 noodles over the meat sauce.
    3. Spread with half the ricota cheese mixture.
    4. Top with 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese slices.
    5. Repeat until finish.
    6. Sprinkle parmesan cheese on top.
