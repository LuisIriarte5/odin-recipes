Lasagna

Description

Everybody-s favorite! You can not miss with a good lasagna. It is not the easiest to prepare, but it is always worth it he effort. Be ready and have patience because it will take you around 3 hours to cook it.

Let-s do it!

Ingredients

This is a very customizable dish, but the essencials are the following:

  • Meat: Ground beef. You can add italian sausage too.
  • Cheese: Recommended parmesan, parlsley and ricotta.
  • Sauce: Marinara sauce will be used. You can buy it or you can do it yourself.
